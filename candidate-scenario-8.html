PROPOSED DESIGN — Candidate BGV Revamp Form: Exit Flow — Step 1 (Scenario 8)
State: Candidate clicked "I don't have preferred documents" on any section (e.g., Permanent Address or PAN). A modal appears — Step 1: Reasoning. The candidate selects a reason from a dropdown, optionally provides more detail, and optionally sets an ETA for when they expect to have the documents. The date field shows a validation error because the entered date is in the past.
Engineering note: This is a 2-step modal flow. Step 1 fields: (1) "Why do you not have the documents?" — dropdown, required. Options: Lost the documents, Serving notice period, Company shut down, Documents never issued, Other. (2) "Explain the reason in detail" — free text, optional. (3) "When can you expect the documents?" — date picker, optional. Validation: ETA cannot be in the past. On "Next" → proceed to Step 2 (Add alternate documents). All 3 fields are saved to the candidate's check record.
