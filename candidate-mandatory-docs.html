I don't have preferred documents
Scenario: Candidate clicks "I don't have preferred documents" on an employment or education section. A 2-step modal appears: (1) Reasoning — candidate explains why they don't have the document, provides a reason and ETA. (2) Add alternate documents — candidate can upload other accepted docs. This is NOT a hard block. The candidate can proceed without the preferred document.
