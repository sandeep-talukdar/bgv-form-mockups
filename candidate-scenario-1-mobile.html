PROPOSED DESIGN — Candidate BGV Revamp Form: Mandatory Document Enforcement (Scenario 1: Homepage) (Mobile)
State: Candidate lands on BGV form. Right panel shows "Required documents" per check type. Preferred = hero document (top of hierarchy, configured by super admin). Acceptable = all collected documents for that check. The homepage shows what the candidate needs upfront.
Engineering note: The "Preferred" and "Acceptable" labels are driven by the BGV Form Config API. For each check type: preferred = the hero document (position 0 in hierarchy, always mandatory). acceptable = all other collected documents. If no hero doc is configured (e.g. address), show "Acceptable" only. Identity shows all 5 document types that are collected.
